FAQ

Common questions

What does the per-sq-ft rate include?

Materials, labour, project management, a dedicated site engineer and warranty as per your package. Compound wall, plan approvals and borewell are optional add-ons so you pay only for what you need.

How do payments work?

Payments are linked to construction milestones — foundation, slab, brickwork, finishing. You pay after each stage is completed and verified, never in advance for the whole project.

Do you help with plan sanction and approvals?

Yes. We assist with BBMP / BDA / panchayat plan sanction through our partner network so the process is hassle-free for you.

Can I customise the package?

Absolutely. Each package has a wallet amount for tiles, fittings and finishes — you choose the brands and designs within (or above) that budget.

Do you take up renovation or only new construction?

Both. We handle additional floors, structural repairs, interiors and compound walls along with full new builds.

Which areas of Bangalore do you cover?

We currently focus on East and North Bangalore — Whitefield, Hoodi, KR Puram, Hoskote, Devanahalli and surrounding areas — and take up projects across the city case by case.
